About me.

I am an assistant research fellow in SDU-ANU Joint Science College at Shandong University, Weihai in China since 2022. Previously, I was a Ph.D. student from 2017-2021 at the University of Lethbridge in Alberta, Canada under the supervision of Drs. Habiba Kadiri and Nathan Ng. My interest mainly lies in analytic number theory.
